Teaching Activities

Courses Taught at Kastamonu University

OMB 559 - Remote Sensing Applications in Forestry

Graduate Level | Spring 2025 | Forest Engineering Dept.

Advanced concepts of remote sensing, focusing on LiDAR technology, change detection, and machine learning applications in forest ecosystem monitoring.

DKB 109 - Computer Technologies and Programming

Undergraduate | Fall 2025 | Dept. of Nature Conservation and Biodiversity

Introduction to computer hardware, software, and fundamental programming logic (Python) specifically tailored for biodiversity data analysis.

OMB 331 - Professional English

Undergraduate | Fall 2024 | Forest Engineering Dept.

Focuses on academic reading, technical terminology in forestry, and effective communication skills for international scientific contexts.

PEY 318 - Remote Sensing

Undergraduate | Spring 2023-2024 | Landscape Architecture Dept.

Basics of satellite imagery analysis, spectral signatures, and land use/land cover mapping for landscape planning.

OMB 327 - Digital Terrain Design

Undergraduate | Fall 2023-2024 | Forest Engineering Dept.

Integration of CAD and GIS technologies for terrain modeling, digital mapping, and spatial analysis in forestry.
